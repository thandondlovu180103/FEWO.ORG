Our Programs

  • Breath work, Meditation and Physical Movement

    • We host community events where we create awareness about the importance of meditation,breathing and physical exercise as a means of reducing symptoms of depression, anxiety and stress.

  • Access to substance abuse treatment

    • Support services aimed at preventing substance abuse and facilitating recovery.

    Offering counseling, mentorship, and community programs to help individuals make healthy choices and maintain sobriety.

  • Book clubs

    • This is to create a culture of reading amongst youth ages 10-18. Using creativity to heal past traumas, enforce a culture of emotional intelligence and independent thinking.

  • Business skills workshops

    • Empowering older youth ages 18-35 with entrepreneurial skills and knowledge to promote self-sufficiency.

  • Charity walks and tournaments

    • Hosting engaging physical activities that unite the community while raising funds for mental health and substance abuse programs.

  • Meadowlands Substance Abuse Forum

    • We work through the forum to host events that support parents,Community members,school children and substance users through: • School Safety Programs • Parent and Caregiver workshops • Parent and caregiver support groups. • Hotspot interventions • Awareness campaigns.
