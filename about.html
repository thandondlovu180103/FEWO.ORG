About FEWO

Feminine Excellence Women's Organisation is a registered NPO based in Meadowlands/ Dobsonville Soweto. She was founded in 2021 by 3 women who are passionate about the wellness of their Community members.

Her objectives are to create a safe drug and crime free environment where youth can heal from past traumas,work creatively to make their dreams come true, live healthier lives physically and mentally. We do this through events,group sessions and events where we create mental health awareness as well as channels for Substance abuse prevention and treatment.

We are proud to serve the Meadowlands/Dobsonville community and work closely with local partners to make a lasting impact on the lives of our members.
