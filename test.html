About Us

FEWO is a non-profit organisation dedicated to raising mental health awareness, providing access to treatment, and preventing substance use and abuse in Soweto. We empower youth and women through reading clubs, business workshops, and creative healing programs to promote emotional intelligence and self-sufficiency.

Our Programs

Book Clubs

Encouraging a culture of reading and independent thinking among youth.

Business Workshops

Equipping older youth with entrepreneurial and business skills for self-sufficiency.

Healing Through Creativity

Providing safe spaces for emotional healing and creative expression.
